Why visit the OPD Instant Clinic?

The clinic has specialists, Family physician available to both assess and offer immediate treatment in emergency cases.

The OPD Instant Clinic is a one-stop service facility. Patients can be in and out in under one hour.

Instant OPD Clinic

The clinic is able to assist patients with non-severe general health issues. Such conditions include headaches, stomach aches, constipation, the flu, and sore throat, for example. The clinic can also provide medical care for cuts and wounds cases which require quick and immediate suturing.

Certain vaccinations can be given at the clinic for adults, such as the influenza vaccination or the shingles vaccination.

The Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

YHL is managed by the Section of Critical Care Medicine to provide 24-hour care for patients with various life-threatening conditions. Supported by the latest medical technology and equipment, our coordinated ICU team of experienced critical care specialists, qualified nurses, and select medical specialists work in close cooperation to provide fast and efficient diagnosis, treatment and monitoring of each patient

Vaccination Center

Immunization is a global health and development success story, saving millions of lives every year. Vaccines reduce risks of getting a disease by working with your body’s natural defenses to build protection. When you get a vaccine, your immune system responds. In YHL vaccination center we provide vaccines for all ages recommended by Government of Bangladesh.

Patient Services Overview

Our patients are served by over 90 nurses and more than 50 doctors and dentists. Covering 23 sub-specialties, which should be able to provide quality care in all medical specialties including Thoracic and neurosurgery.

The hospital features a 24/7 hour Emergency Room; 3 Modular operating theatres; Three types of intensive care units (adult, pediatric, neo-natal ICU); and HDU , Sports Medicine and rehabilitation center. , Dialysis center.

Surgical facilities include Gynecology and obstructive, General surgery, Laparoscopy surgery, Orthopedic, Neuro and spine surgery, ENT surgery, Thoracic surgery, Plastic surgery Upcoming Operation theaters for cardiac catheterization lab, specifically set up for cardiac surgery.

The non-Invasive procedures -Endoscopy, Arthroscopy, lithotripsy, Bronchoscopy, Laryngoscopy, Colonoscopy are available almost every day.
