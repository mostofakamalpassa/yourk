York Hospital Ltd.

A Healthcare Service Provider

The journey of York Hospital started with the concept of Boutique hospital to extend the service one steps beyond the conventional healthcare. Our personalized care to the patient is more focus on dedicated Nursing care and the specialized Doctors supervision to give better treatment.

• 5 stories building with total 31,500 SQF area with lake side parking.

• Hospital is under surveillance of CC camera.
